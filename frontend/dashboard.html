Prometheus / Grafana Setup

The /metrics endpoint exposes Prometheus-compatible metrics. A sample prometheus.yml and Grafana dashboard are in the monitoring/ directory. 

scrape_configs:
  - job_name: aegis_moderation
    static_configs:
      - targets: [localhost:8000]
    metrics_path: /metrics
